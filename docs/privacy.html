Scope

This Policy covers routerhaus.com and pages that link to it (the “Services”). We target U.S. users. If you are outside the U.S., your rights may differ.

Information We Collect

1) You provide

  • Contact/Consult: name, email, company, phone, message content.
  • Quiz inputs: space type/size, devices, ISP tier, usage goals.
  • Support: troubleshooting details, files you submit.

2) Automatic

  • Device & usage: IP address, browser/OS, pages viewed, time on page, referrer, interactions.
  • Cookies/local storage: preferences (e.g., theme), analytics, and—if enabled—ads/attribution.

3) From partners

  • Analytics/ads: traffic measurement, performance, attribution.
  • Affiliate networks: outbound click identifiers used to credit referrals; we do not receive your card or order details from the merchant.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, personalize, and improve recommendations and the Services.
  • Respond to inquiries, schedule consults, and provide support.
  • Security, fraud prevention, debugging, and performance measurement.
  • Service communications; marketing with consent or as permitted (opt-out anytime).
  • Compliance with law; enforcing terms; creating de-identified and aggregated insights.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

  • Strictly necessary: security, core functions.
  • Analytics: site usage and performance.
  • Advertising/Attribution: measure partner link performance.

You can block cookies in your browser; some features may not work. We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required—see below.

How We Disclose Information

  • Service providers (processors): hosting, security, analytics, email, scheduling, error monitoring—bound to use data only for us.
  • Affiliate/attribution partners: limited identifiers when you click outbound links to merchants.
  • Advertising partners: if used, for measurement/cross-context behavioral advertising (opt-out available).
  • Business transfers: merger, financing, acquisition, or dissolution.
  • Legal: to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, and protect rights and safety.

We do not sell personal information for money. We may “share” certain data for targeted advertising as defined by state law; you can opt out.

Your Rights (U.S.)

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, delete, correct, opt out of targeted advertising (“sharing”), and obtain portability.

  • Submit a request: email privacy@routerhaus.com with subject “Privacy Request”.
  • Verification: we may request information to verify your identity. California: authorized agents must provide proof of authority and we may verify the consumer directly.
  • Timing: we typically respond within 45 days (may extend once by 45 days; we’ll notify you).
  • Appeals (CO/CT/VA): if we deny your request, email us with subject “Privacy Appeal”. We’ll respond within 45 days with reasons and how to contact your Attorney General if you disagree.
  • Non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Your Privacy Choices

  • Marketing emails: use the unsubscribe link or email us.
  • Analytics/Ads cookies: manage in your browser or via any consent banner we provide.
  • Do Not Sell/Share: email us with subject “Do Not Sell/Share”. Where applicable, we honor GPC signals as an opt-out for the browser sending the signal.

Do Not Track (DNT) signals are not standardized; we currently respond to GPC instead of DNT.
