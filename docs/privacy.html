Scope & Controller

Information We Collect

2) Automatically collected

3) From third parties

  • Affiliate networks when you follow a link to a seller (e.g., anonymous or pseudonymous click IDs).

  • Provide and improve recommendations, products, and Services.
  • Respond to inquiries, schedule consults, and provide support.

  • Send service messages; send marketing with consent or as allowed by law (you can opt out any time).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms; de-identify and aggregate data for statistics.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use necessary cookies and local storage for core functions (e.g., theme), and—if enabled—analytics and advertising/attribution cookies.

  • Strictly necessary: security, basic site features.

Browser settings allow you to block cookies. Blocking may impact functionality. We respond to Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required by law.

We do not sell personal information for money. Under some state laws, “sharing” for targeted advertising may occur; see State Notices.
